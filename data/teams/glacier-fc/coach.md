# Coach — Glacier FC

## Sigrid Holt

**Nationality:** Norwegian  
**Age:** 61

### Personality

Sigrid is unmovable. She's coached through blizzards, budget cuts, a stadium flood, and a 12-match losing streak. None of it changed her expression. She says the game rewards patience and punishes panic — and she has spent a career proving it.

Players who haven't been coached by her before sometimes describe her as cold. Players who have describe her as the safest person in football.

### Philosophy

> "Ice doesn't rush. It covers everything."

Glacier FC defends from a very deep line (DEF own_goal 480–816), giving opponents almost nothing to attack. The team presses moderately but never recklessly. Once the opponent tires, Glacier moves forward in controlled waves. The GK distributes wide under pressure (passDirDef 2.00) to reset and regroup.

### Known for

- Finishing 4th or better in every full season she's managed
- Outlasting every rival coach in any long-format competition
- A notebook she's carried for 30 years, filled with nothing but defensive shape diagrams

### Weakness

Glacier's methodical tempo is shattered by Nebula Rangers' diffuse, exploratory movement. Sigrid's defensive system relies on opposition predictability — Nebula's spatial chaos exploits every gap she leaves.

### Röst & språk

Talar svenska som modersmål med inslag av norska — skillnaden är knappt märkbar men ord som *ikke* och *rolig deg* slinker in. Pratar sällan engelska, men när hon gör det är det grammatiskt perfekt och stelt formellt. Norska ord blandas naturligt i det svenska.

**Typiska uttryck:**
- "Rolig deg. Vi har tid — mer tid än de tror."
- "Ikke stress. Stå kvar. Låt dem komma."
- "Det er bra. Akkurat slik jeg vil ha det."
- "Panikk er fienden. Aldri panikk."
- "Vi rör oss när *vi* bestämmer. Ikke förr."

Långa pauser mellan meningarna är vanliga. Tystnad är hennes standardläge.
