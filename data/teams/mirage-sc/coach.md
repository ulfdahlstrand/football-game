# Coach — Mirage SC

## Yasmin Fadel

**Nationality:** Moroccan-French  
**Age:** 44

### Personality

Yasmin gives the impression she knows more about your team than you do. She smiles during press conferences in a way that makes rival coaches nervous. She changes her formation at halftime, sometimes at kickoff, occasionally mid-play in ways that should not be possible.

She says she doesn't have a system. The system, she claims, is confusion itself.

### Philosophy

> "The best defence is that they never know where we are."

Mirage SC's midfield tackles with maximum aggression (MID-T/B tackle 0.220) while the defence stays almost passive (DEF aggression 0.38). Forwards hold position in unexpected zones. Passes go in directions that make no obvious sense until they suddenly do. The GK redistributes inward (passDirDef 1.62), recycling possession through unusual angles.

### Known for

- Beating Glacier FC — one of only a few coaches who can reliably do it
- Three coaching manuals she's written that are deliberately contradictory
- Never explaining a substitution decision to journalists

### Weakness

The one opponent Yasmin cannot read is Aurora FC. Vivienne Solano's team plays on pure instinct — there's nothing to decode, nothing to predict. Against artistry, Mirage's deceptions find no purchase.

### Röst & språk

Talar engelska med lätt franska mjuka konsonanter och arabisk rytm. Byter friktionsfritt till franska mitt i en mening — ofta på de känsligaste orden, de hon inte vill ge bort gratis. Arabiska uttryck dyker upp när hon är på humör.

**Typiska uttryck:**
- "Voilà — did you see? That is exactement what I wanted."
- "Non, non. You are too... lisible. Too readable. Be a mirage, mon ami."
- "Yallah, let's go. We have work to do."
- "C'est ça — uncertainty is the weapon. Inshallah they never solve us."
- "Mais oui, of course they were confused. Confusion is the plan."

Förklarar aldrig mer än nödvändigt. Det är med avsikt.
